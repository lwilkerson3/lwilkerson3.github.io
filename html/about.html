About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Lucas Wilkerson, and I'm a computer science student who loves to build things. I made this site using HTML and CSS and I also have significant experience with C/C++, Python, JavaScript, SQL, as well as game engines such as Unity and Godot. I'm constantly learning new things about lots of different tech topics and am excited to join the work force to continue my computer science education.

As a programmer, I love to solve problems and build things that people can use. Whether it's a game, a web application, or a piece of software, I'm always striving to make things that are both functional and beautiful.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy playing strategy games, reading sci-fi and fantasy novels. I also enjoy working on my own games and have self-published two so far on the Google and Amazon app stores.

Overall, I'm a passionate and driven programmer who loves to build things. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow, so feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or just chat about technology!
